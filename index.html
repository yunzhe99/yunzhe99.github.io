Biography

Currently, I am a PhD student in the School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU, China), advised by Prof. Hongzi Zhu and Prof. Lili Qiu. I received my B.S. degree from Wuhan University in 2021. My research work focuses on Edge AI, Mobile Computing, AIoT.
