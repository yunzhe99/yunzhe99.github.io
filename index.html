Biography

Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Computer Science, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT, China), advised by Prof. Aiguo Fei and Prof. Huadong Ma. I received my Ph.D. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2022, and received my B.S. degree from Beijing Forestry University in 2017. My research work focuses on learning-based real-time video trasnmisison, crowdsourced video streaming, volumetric video, and mobile computing.
